Points are earned through the following ways:

Attendance
2 points for attending an event

Event Placement

1st place: 8 points
2nd: 6 points
3rd: 5 points
4th: 4 points
Top 50% of field: 2 points
Top 75% of field: 1 point

Starting Tag Bonus
+1 point for each player at the event with a worse (higher number) tag than yours
Maximum of 6 points

Tag #1 Bonus

+2 points if you start the event with tag #1

Beat Your Tag Bonus

Players earn bonus points based on how much they outperform their starting position at the event:
Players are ranked at the start of the event based on their tag among attendees
Players are ranked again based on event results

Improvement is calculated as starting rank minus finishing rank

Bonus points are awarded as follows:

Improve by 1–2 positions: +1 point
3–4 positions: +2 points
5 or more positions: +3 points

Ties
Tied players at the end of the event share placement/points
